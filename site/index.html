Edinburgh Hacklab

Edinburgh Hacklab is part of the worldwide movement of Hackerspaces.

Shared spaces for people who mess around with technology for fun. By creating a shared space to do this, we provide access to better facilities than we could each have at home, as well as opportunities to collaborate, learn, and socialise. We are not a coworking space.

How to become a member

You don’t need to be a member to come along to our open nights, but if you want to be part of Hacklab and get 24 hour access, join us!

Come Visit

We're open to the public every Tuesday, 7PM till late. If you can’t make Tuesday, feel free to ask around.

Our Location

Edinburgh Hacklab is located at Summerhall, just at the East edge of the Meadows.
