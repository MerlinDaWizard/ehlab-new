Arduino is a micro-controller platform designed for ease of use and
learning. It allows the creation of electronically controlled projects,
whether it be simple blinking lights, a robot or a music generator.

This workshop is aimed at beginners. You don't needs any previous
electronics or programming knowledge or experience. Topics covered
include:

- An introduction to the Arduino
- Using electronic components to build circuits
- Input and output
- Generating sound
- Expanding your Arduino
- And more!

Price: £45 including Ardunio, £25 excluding Ardunio.

All the electronic components will be provided. If you already have an
Arduino you can bring that along and just purchase a kit.
